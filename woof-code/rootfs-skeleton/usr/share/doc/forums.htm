Discussion forums and chat

Main Puppy Linux Discussion Forum

The main Puppy Linux Discussion Forum is hosted by team member rockedge. It started at the end of 2019 and has been the "go to" forum board for much of the community in recent times.

This is the place where all the latest discussion happens so if you need help with any issue to do with Puppy, old or new, drop on by and someone will help you as soon as they can.

Link:-

forum.puppylinux.com

Puppy Linux Old-Discussion Forum

Thanks to the generosity of John Murga, who also has developed a version of Puppy that fits in a 50MB credit-card CD, that he hosted this forum. This was our main Puppy Discussion Forum since 2005 until 2020.

Unfortunately John Murga passed away in May 2020 and the old forum is preserved as his legacy to the Puppy community. It is now read only. There is a wealth of information at the old forum, which might not be even found on newer forums, and is definitely worth browsing.

Link:-

oldforum.puppylinux.com
